Joel M Turner operates [joelmturner.com](https://joelmturner.com). This page describes what information the site collects, which third-party services are involved, and what choices you have.

## What this site collects

When you visit joelmturner.com, limited technical information may be recorded to keep the site running and to understand how it is used:

- **Page views** — anonymized visit counts via GoatCounter (production only)
- **Error reports** — client-side errors sent to Sentry in production to help fix bugs
- **Hosting logs** — standard request logs from [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) (IP address, browser, pages requested, timestamps)

This is a static personal site. There is no account system and no forms that collect email or other personal details on the public site.

## Cookies and local storage

- **GoatCounter** may set a session cookie to distinguish unique visits. See [GoatCounter's privacy documentation](https://www.goatcounter.com/help/privacy) for details.
- **Theme preference** is stored in your browser's `localStorage` (light/dark mode) and is not sent to a server.
- This site does not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

You can block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect analytics counting but should not prevent you from reading the site.

## What we do not do

- Sell or rent visitor data
- Run targeted advertising
- Require an account to browse the site
- Knowingly collect information from children under 13

## Your choices

- Use browser privacy settings or extensions to block analytics scripts and third-party requests
- Clear cookies and site data at any time through your browser
- For GoatCounter-specific options, see [their privacy help page](https://www.goatcounter.com/help/privacy)

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes take effect when posted on this page with an updated effective date.
